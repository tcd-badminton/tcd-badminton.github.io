Welcome!

This is the official TCD Badminton website. Here you can find everything you need to know about our fantastic club and catch up on all the exciting things that are happening within the club.

A quick intro

The TCD Badminton Club is a long standing member of the Dublin University Central Athletic Club (DUCAC) which is the governing body for sport clubs at Trinity College Dublin. We cater for every level of player and the club has enjoyed a rich history of success both on and off the court.



Over the course of 2016/2017 our new dedicated committee will be aiming to ensure all aspects of the club build upon the success of recent years.

The new club year kicks off in Freshers week where our committee members will be on hand in front square to sign you (new and returning students/staff/alumni) up to the club. We'll add you to our mailing list to keep you up to date with important notices about the club and events happening throughout the year.

You should also like our Facebook page and join the official TCD Badminton group to keep up to date with all the exciting things that are happening.
